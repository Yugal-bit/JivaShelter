About Us

Welcome to JivaShelter!

We are students of Vishwakarma Institute of Information Technology, and we have created this website for peoples to search and adopt a pet for themself.

Animals in shelters often suffer from stress and loneliness due to overcrowding and lack of attention. By adopting, you give them a chance to experience love and happiness in a caring home.

Our website connects you to various shelter homes and people in Pune with animals available for adoption. We provide you the connection to the audience if there is someone you need to be adopted.

Thanks for visiting our website.

PAWS CARE, PUNE

PAWS CARE is a nonprofit organization dedicated to enhancing the lives of street animals in our community. Founded by Manuel Viegas, they provide onsite treatments for injured and sick animals at no cost. Our mission is to "Make a Difference" by implementing community-driven initiatives and innovative projects focused on animal welfare. We also prioritize education and outreach to raise awareness about the needs of street animals. With strong support from the local community, PAWS CARE continues to grow and positively impact the lives of animals in need.

Porwal Road, Lohegao.

Sinhagad Animal Welfare and Care (SAWCT)

Sinhagad Animal Welfare and Care (SAWCT) is an organization that is committed to rescuing and rehabilitating stray and injured animals. They have succeeded in rescuing, treating, fostering, neutering, and finding loving homes for numerous stray and homeless animals with the collaborative help of other major NGOs in Pune. To date, they have successfully facilitated the adoption of more than 500 animals. Although their primary work is based around Sinhgad Road, they have extended their support and services to animals in Kothrud, Warje, Sahakar Nagar, Katraj, Kondhwa, Mulshi, and Peth areas as needed.

Pandurang Industrial area, Nanded.

Animal Rescue Trust (ART)

Animal Rescue Trust (ART) was established with the mission of providing free, permanent housing for rescued and severely injured animals. Initially, they focused on creating a retirement home for dogs that were unlikely to be adopted. Over time, they have expanded to offer high-quality medical services for street animals at extremely affordable rates. Now, they provide a wide range of services aimed at animal care, reducing human-animal conflicts, and advocating for animal welfare both legally and socially.

Manjri Khurd.

RESQ Charitable trust

RESQ is a non-governmental organization founded in2007 by Neha Panchamiya, dedicated to providing emergency aid and rehabilitation for animals in distress across Maharashtra, India. Over the past15 years, RESQ has impacted the lives of over10,000 animals from more than200 species, focusing on wildlife rescue, medical care, and community education to prevent human-animal conflict. The organization operates a Wildlife Transit Treatment Centre in Pune in collaboration with the Maharashtra Forest Department and emphasizes the importance of coexistence between wildlife and local communities through proactive outreach and policy advocacy. With a team of over60 employees, RESQ is committed to evolving and adapting its strategies to meet the changing needs of the environment and the animals it serves.

Pune, Maharashtra.
