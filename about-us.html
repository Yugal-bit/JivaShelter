About Us

Welcome to JivaShelter!

We are students of Vishwakarma Institute of Information technology and this is our website for helping stray animals to get adopted.

Animals in shelters often suffer from stress and loneliness due to overcrowding and lack of attention. By adopting, you give them a chance to experience love and happiness in a caring home.

Our website will connect you to various shelter homes and peoples in Pune with animals to adopt.
